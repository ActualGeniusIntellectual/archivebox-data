part  of BEA    The U.S. economy is complex, diverse, dynamic. To understand it, Americans need the Bureau of Economic  Analysis. We take the economy's vital signs – how big, how fast, how it's  changing. From national  indicators to local insights, our statistics help businesses, leaders, and  people everywhere see trends, find opportunities, and make smart decisions. If you're passionate about a career that makes a  difference, take a closer  look at BEA and (https://www.bea.gov/about/working-at-bea/how-to-apply) apply today . Explore      Room for Everyone  to Shine      Though our influence in the world of economics looms large, we're a relatively small bureau. At BEA, you'll have the chance to really know and work with leaders. You can volunteer, join groups, make friends, and always keep growing and learning.
