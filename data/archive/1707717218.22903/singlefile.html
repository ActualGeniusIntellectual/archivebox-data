BEARFACTS

BEA Regional Fact Sheet about personal income and gross domestic product (GDP).

  • Area Type
  • Video: Finding Your Data

To view your customized fact sheet, select an area type.

Factsheet State

State BEARFACTS are based on the state estimates published on March 31, 2023 for personal income and March 31, 2023 for GDP by state.

Factsheet County

County BEARFACTS are based on the county estimates published on November 16, 2023 for personal income and December 7, 2023 for local area GDP.

Factsheet Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA)

Metropolitan Statistical Areas BEARFACTS are based on the county estimates published on November 16, 2022 for personal income and December 7, 2023 for local area GDP.
